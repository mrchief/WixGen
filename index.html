    WixGen

    Wix toolkit is awesome! Authoring a .wxs file by hand - tedious!

    It is Xml and thus verbose by nature. Moreover, the first time learning is challenging and fun. Once you know the innards of it, it becomes quite boring.

    Many times, you just want to point to a folder, specify few basic settings and wave a magic wand - and wish a .wxs file to be created!

    Well WixGen attempts to be the magic wand in the process. Before you get greedy with the newly acquired power, you must remember that is just a limited edition for now!

    Other than generating a basic .wxs file, it doesn't offer much. This fulfills my basic needs for most cases (i.e. point-n-click wxs generation).

    If you like the idea, then I welcome you to enhance this by adding more capabilities (such as supporting a desktop app?)! Feel free to fork and send a pull request. Or send me a PM to become a contributor!
